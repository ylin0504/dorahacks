# **Why DoraHacks?**

## **Comprehensive Platform and Infrastructure**

- The [DoraHacks platform](https://dorahacks.io/) was built with user experience in mind, and boasts a wide range of features designed to support projects and developers, including: BUIDLs, Hackathons, Grants, and Ideas
- Round-the-clock technical support is available from the DoraHacks team, with over 10 years of experience
- Cutting-edge technological toolkits such as MACI/a-MACI and Quadratic Voting solve the problems associated with community governance

## **Global Community**

- With over 147,000 active builders, the DoraHacks community is a powerhouse of global talent
- Our partnerships with communities and established projects in the technology sector, along with our social media influence, give users access to a large network of like-minded professionals, entrepreneurs, and investors

## **Know-How**

- Founded in 2014, DoraHacks possesses a wealth of experience running hackathons and act as consultants throughout the planning and execution stages
- Proven experts at coordinating offline (in-person) hackathons, we have also supported over 500 hackathons organized using our online platform
- We further support activities through social media promotion, demos, and workshops
- We know how to help an ecosystem grow their developer community and successfully engage users
- Dora Dojo is an open-source community initiative by DoraHacks focusing on frontier technologies. It hosts live sessions and workshops for knowledge sharing and community engagement, catering to learners and tech contributors

## **Funding Support**

- Promising early-stage projects are often selected for investment or receive support in the form of hackathon prizes
- To date, over 4000 projects and more than 5300 startup teams from the DoraHacks community have received over $40 million in grants and other forms of financial support from supporters worldwide
