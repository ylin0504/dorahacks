# **BUIDL**

- A project listed on the DoraHacks platform

# **MACI**

- Minimal Anti-Collusion Infrastructure is a voting system in which the identities and voting choices of participants are hidden from the public  [[Video]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tpiDH5qdDXY)

**a-MACI**
- Anonymous MACI takes privacy to the next level by providing anonymity for voters even from the organizers themselves

**Voice Credit** 

- voting tickets used by voters in community voting and judging

# **QF**

 - Quadratic Funding is a crowd-funding mechanism that amplifies available resources by inviting community members to make donations that act as votes on where to allocate funds

# **Matching Pool**

- The pool of funds provided by the organizer to be distributed according to the outcome of community voting

# **Progressive Tax**

- A feature of DoraHacksâ€™ Quadratic Funding whereby each additional vote for the same option costs increasingly more resources, resulting in a more equitable community voting experience
